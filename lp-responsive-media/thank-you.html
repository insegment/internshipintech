FOCUS ON THE NETWORK TRAFFIC MONITORING

Arbor’s does not focus on specific threats or points of attack, but on networks themselves. No matter how much the threats and attackers have changed, security still comes down to consistent network traffic monitoring and complete network visibility. Arbor provides your organization with the network intelligence it needs to identify, understand and stop external and internal threats to your business.

NETWORK VISIBILITY WITH NETFLOW

Arbor Networks NSI sits inside the network and collects network intelligence via IP flow data (e.g., NetFlow, sFlow, etc.), delivering pervasive and cost-effective application and network visibility. As NSI gathers this information, it learns normal traffic and routing behavior across hundreds of routers and thousands of interfaces, and correlates network visibility with topology data to build logical data models. Armed with this information, NSI notifies network operations staff of significant changes to the network (a.k.a. network anomalies) due to misconfiguration, equipment failure or a DDoS attack.

DEEP INSIGHT WITH FULL PACKET CAPTURE

Arbor Networks SA solution gives organizations an unprecedented and detailed view of attacks in any captured network traffic, allowing users to analyze data at the speed of their thoughts versus waiting for long queries. Powerful visualizations display network intelligence from multiple perspectives (attacker, target, location or attack type)—enabling analysts to quickly assess the security posture of the organization. Once an indicator of compromise has been identified, SA provides the analyst with actionable network intelligence, allowing confirmation of the details and extent of the attack.
